More Than Just
Bounce Houses

REMEDY Rex's Bounce Houses & Inflatables is a family-owned and operated business built on one simple idea from Anthony Stagliano, Jr. — events should be more than just gatherings; they should be unforgettable experiences.

What started as an idea to offer more than just traditional bounce houses quickly grew into a full-service inflatable and event entertainment company designed to bring excitement, energy, and lasting memories to every celebration.

Anthony envisioned a brand that went beyond the ordinary — combining high-quality inflatables, interactive games, slides, obstacle courses, and event-focused customer service to create moments families and communities would remember for years to come.
